Folding Instructions

Follow these steps to create your hexaflexagon

Back to Generator

Step 1: Cut Out the Template

Cut out the hexaflexagon shape along the outer solid lines.

Step 2: First Fold

Fold along the long horizontal dashed line in the middle.

Step 3: Second Fold

Fold along the second line as indicated in the first image above.

Step 4: Third Fold

Fold along the third line as indicated in the first image above to the back.

Step 5: Check Your Progress

Now, you should see one of your images displayed as shown below.

Step 6: Final Assembly

Fold the white triangle flap inward along the dashed line, and then glue it to the other white triangle underneath.

Step 7: Finished!

You're done! Now you can flex your hexaflexagon to reveal the different faces with your images.
